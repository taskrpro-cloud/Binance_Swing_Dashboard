📖 Scalp guide — read before trading
⏱Hold time: 3–15 minutes. Maximum 30 minutes. If TP not hit in 30 min, exit manually — the signal has expired.
🎯Entry: Wait for the 1m EMA crossover candle to fully close. Enter at market on the next candle open. Never enter mid-candle.
🛑Stop: 0.25% from entry. Set immediately after position opens. Do not move it. The wicks you see are normal — your stop must survive them.
📊All 6 green required. 5/6 is not enough for a scalp. The noise at 1m level punishes marginal setups harshly.
🔁After a loss: Wait 15 minutes minimum. No revenge trades. Check if any signal was borderline before entry.
🚫Hard limits: Max 20 trades/day. Stop if account drops 3% in a day. Max 3–5× leverage.

📖 Intraday guide — 1H/4H timeframe
⏱Hold time: 2–12 hours. Check in every 2–4 hours. Not every 5 minutes. Set alerts, not alarms.
🎯Entry: Enter at the close of a strong 1H candle confirming at a 4H structure level — engulfing body, clear rejection wick, or breakout close.
🛑Stop: Below the swing low that formed at your entry level. Typically 0.5–1% from entry. Wider than scalp — the 4H breathes more.
📊RSI zone: For longs look for RSI 35–52 on 1H (bouncing from low). For shorts look for RSI 50–68 (rolling over from high).
🌙Overnight: Reduce size by 50% if you'll hold through a session close. Funding hits every 8h — factor it into your P&L expectation.
🚫Hard limits: Max 5 trades/day. Max 3× leverage for intraday. Stop if −2% on the day.

📖 Swing guide — 4H/Daily timeframe
⏱Hold time: 1–3 days. Check charts twice a day max. Checking more often leads to early exits from normal noise.
📍Entry location: Only enter near a key daily level — prev day high/low or clear 4H structure. Never chase price mid-range.
🛑Stop: 1× daily ATR below the swing low at your entry level. Wide enough to survive 1–2 daily candles of normal movement.
🎯Target: Next daily level or 1.5–2× your stop distance. Do not move TP closer once set. Big moves require patience.
💰Funding: You will pay it 3–6 times per trade. At extreme funding (>0.1%) a 3-day hold can cost 0.3–0.6% extra. Factor this in.
📅Frequency: Expect 2–5 quality setups per week. Boredom trading is the #1 killer at this timeframe. No setup = no trade.
🚫Hard limits: Max 2× leverage for swing. Stop if −3% on the week. One trade at a time.
