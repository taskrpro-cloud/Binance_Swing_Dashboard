How to use this dashboard
⏱Hold time: 4 hours to 3 days. This is not scalping. Give trades room to breathe — check once or twice a day, not every minute.
📍Entry location: Only enter near a key level — previous day high/low, or a 4H structure zone. Never chase price in the middle of a range.
🛑Stop loss: Place below the swing low that formed at your entry level, or 1× ATR below entry. Wide enough to survive normal volatility.
🎯Take profit: Target the next daily level or 1.5–2× your stop distance. Do not move TP closer once set. Let it run.
⚠️Time-based exit: If price hasn't moved 50% toward TP within 24 hours, consider exiting. A stalled trade is capital stuck at risk.
💤Frequency: Expect 2–5 quality setups per week, not per day. Waiting is part of the strategy. Boredom trading kills accounts.
🔔Alerts: Enable alerts above — you'll get a notification when 4, 5, or all 6 conditions align. You don't need to watch the screen.
